The why

“I'm a Type 1 diabetic. I've lost my right big toe, and others — fourteen foot surgeries. I know how lonely it gets: the insulin, the bills, the recovery, the 24/7 of it. So we built the thing I needed, and the thing they need. It costs them nothing. Give people what they need, not what you have.”

— Donovan, founder

Diabetes humbles the strongest person in the room. Nobody beats it alone — everybody needs the swarm. Your gift is the swarm.
